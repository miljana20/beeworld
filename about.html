PROFESIONAL STAFF

Beekeeping is one of the noblest branches of agriculture, so we have to treat it that way. Like a vineyard, bees do not need a master but a servant. We train our professional staff that way. Our Beekeepers are dedicated lovers of nature and animals - in our case bees. Calmness is needed to work with bees. Our Beekeepers possess that virtue as well. We must emphasize that beekeeping, although a wonderful profession, requires great sacrifices. Every beekeeper must be ready for the daily care of bees, bee stings, and that his earnings depend on nature. Therefore, our recommendation is that you engage in beekeeping solely out of love.

WHAT ARE WE DOING?

As we mentioned earlier, beekeeping requires great sacrifices, especially if you are a big and quality entrepreneur. Everyone needs attention, including bees. Bees, although brilliant and hardy creatures, need someone to take care of them from time to time. That is why they have our beekeepers. In addition to throwing honey, the beekeeper is obliged to make sure that his bees are not hungry during the scarce seasons, then to regularly inspect the apiary and protect it from predators and other dangers (including weather conditions). And last but not least, to protect against diseases and cure them if they occur.

HOW DO WE ACHIEVE THIS?

We have already established what bees need. They need attention, daily care, protection from predators, weather disasters, as well as infectious diseases. But how do we achieve all this? Well, first of all with the techniques we have gained through many years of experience in beekeeping. To begin with, with the quality equipment we provide to our beehives, we protect our little creatures from natural disasters. After that, with selected and tested chemicals and medications, we prevent any kind of disease from coming into contact with our bees. And most important of all, we provide our beehives with daily supervision and care if it is necessary.
